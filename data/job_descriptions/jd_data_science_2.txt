Summary

The Data Scientist is responsible for analyzing complex data to uncover valuable insights, build predictive models, and support data-driven decision-making across the company. You will work closely with various teams to design and implement models that drive business outcomes, optimize processes, and solve problems across departments such as Product, Marketing, and Operations. This role is integral to Deel’s data-driven culture, making it a core function within the company’s broader analytics and data strategy.

Responsibilities

Solve real world problems using applied algorithmic research and Data Science practices
Implement functionality which can be served in production for internal customers as well as external customers
Designing, building and maintaining data sets
Data cleaning
Feature engineering and extraction
Modeling
Experimentation
Online and offline demonstrations of capabilities
Building end-to-end machine learning pipelines
Conduct reproducible research
Collaborate with Engineering, Operations, Product Management and other functions in the company to deliver algorithmic solutions
Apply software engineering practices in our code that implements our research and its infrastructure
Produce high quality, clean, maintainable reproducible research and code


Qualifications

Background and experience in data/backend engineering, ideally in production environments (3+ years) (Mid/Senior Level role)
High proficiency in Python and its data science stack.
Background and hands-on experience (2+ years) in applied algorithmic research, specifically in information retrieval, text processing, computer vision (CV), computational linguistics (NLP) and machine learning
Track record of good written and verbal communication of complex things in a simple way as well as ability to collaborate well with people from different backgrounds and professions
A master’s degree or higher
Experience in NLP is a plus
